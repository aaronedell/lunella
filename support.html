Getting Help

Most questions can be answered in the FAQ section below. If you need additional assistance, please review the troubleshooting steps provided.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I set up the widget?
  1. Open the Lunella app and select your cycle start date
  2. Long-press on your home screen
  3. Tap the '+' button in the top left
  4. Search for 'Lunella'
  5. Select the small widget size
  6. Tap 'Add Widget'
Q: When should I set my cycle start date?

Set the cycle start date to the first day of your OUT phase (the day you remove the ring). The app will automatically track your 7-day OUT period followed by your 21-day IN period.

Q: The widget isn't updating. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  • Open the Lunella app to ensure your cycle start date is set
  • Remove the widget from your home screen and add it again
  • Restart your iPhone
  • Make sure you're running iOS 17.0 or later
Q: What does the open ring vs. closed ring mean?

The widget displays a visual representation of your cycle:

  • Open ring (broken circle): OUT phase - ring is removed (7 days)
  • Closed ring (complete circle): IN phase - ring is inserted (21 days)
Q: How do I change my cycle start date?

Open the Lunella app and use the date picker to select a new cycle start date. The widget will automatically update within a few moments.

Q: Is my data private and secure?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device only. Lunella does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Q: Can I use Lunella as my only form of contraception tracking?

Important: Lunella is a tracking tool only and is not intended as medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always follow the instructions provided by your healthcare provider and the prescribing information for your contraceptive vaginal ring. Consult your doctor if you have any questions about your contraceptive use.

Q: What iOS version do I need?

Lunella requires iOS 17.0 or later.
